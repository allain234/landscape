About Us

At Bike Service, we are dedicated to providing Clean, lube and re-assemble. Brief inspection of bicycle, maximum time spent around 30 minutes. Adjustment of the brakes, derailleurs, hubs, headset, bottom bracket, and minor truing of wheels (no extra tensioning of wheels or major flat spot correction). All bolts are checked for tightness and the chain is lubed.
